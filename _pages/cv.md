    <h3 class="card-title font-weight-medium">Professional Summary</h3>
    <p><strong>Theory-driven robotics researcher with 7+ years of experience building end-to-end robotic systems, from mathematical formulation and algorithm design to sensing, hardware integration, and real-time control.</strong></p>
    <p>My work focuses on <strong>control, optimization, and physical interaction under uncertainty</strong>, with established contributions in <strong>contact modeling</strong>, <strong>trajectory optimization / MPC</strong>, and <strong>adaptive and passivity-based control</strong>. A key strength of my research is combining <strong>deep theoretical development</strong> with <strong>full-stack robotic implementation</strong>, validated on <strong>high-DoF soft and continuum robotic platforms</strong> with <strong>kHz-rate control pipelines</strong>.</p>
    <p>My current direction is toward <strong>deeper foundations for learning, control, and decision-making under uncertainty</strong>, aiming to develop <strong>sample-efficient, physically grounded, and robust methods</strong>. I am the author of <strong>11+ peer‑reviewed publications</strong> and the recipient of multiple competitive <strong>fellowships and research awards</strong>.</p>
    <p><strong>Technical focus</strong>: Control Theory · Optimal Control · Trajectory Optimization · Model Predictive Control · Contact Modeling · Adaptive and Passivity-Based Control · Learning for Control · Decision-Making under Uncertainty · Contact-Rich Manipulation · Real-Time Robotic Systems</p>
