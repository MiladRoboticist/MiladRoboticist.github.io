title: "Herbert P. Haley Fellowship"

I am pleased to have been selected to receive a **Herbert P. Haley Fellowship** for the 2025–2026 academic year from the College of Engineering. This is a one-time merit award granted to a small number of the most highly qualified continuing graduate students within each College, based on the nomination of my academic unit. The fellowship was established at Georgia Tech in honor of Herbert P. Haley (ME 1933) through the generosity of the late Marion Peacock Haley.
